Privacy Policy

Movie Tattoo does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) from users. We do not require account creation to use the app.

The app uses third-party APIs (e.g. The Movie Database) to fetch movie and TV show data, but none of your personal data is shared with these services.

All AI-generated tattoo images are created based on the input you provide and are processed securely. We do not store or track any of your data or images.

We may use analytics tools (e.g. Firebase Analytics) to understand general app usage trends and improve user experience. These tools do not collect personally identifiable information.

If you have any privacy-related concerns, please contact us at tyljacten1996@icloud.com.
